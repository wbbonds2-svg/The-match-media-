/ FAQ

QUICK ANSWERS.

Don't see your question? Contact us and we'll get back to you.

01 — What types of businesses do you work with?
We work primarily with small businesses and nonprofits across Washington State. Restaurants, salons, fitness studios, consultants, retail shops, service providers — if you need a consistent, professional social presence and don't have time to do it yourself, we're a fit.
02 — Do you provide on-site filming for our content?
Yes. We film on-site anywhere in the Puget Sound region — Pierce, King, and Snohomish counties. We bring all equipment and direct the shoot so you know exactly what to do. We also have studio availability for certain content types.
03 — How do you create a social media strategy?
We start with a strategy call to understand your brand, audience, and goals. From there we build a custom content plan — posting cadence, platform focus, content formats, and creative direction — all tailored to your business, not a template.
04 — Are services customizable?
Every plan is custom. We don't sell packages off a shelf. After your strategy call, we put together a plan that fits your content needs, posting frequency, and budget. Pricing starts at $499/month and scales from there.
05 — Do you work with small businesses?
That's exactly who we built this for. Small businesses are our focus — not enterprise brands. We understand the budget constraints, the limited time, and the need for content that feels real, not corporate.
06 — Do you provide filming?
Yes — filming is included in our full-service plans. We come to your location with all equipment, direct the shoot, capture everything we need, and handle all editing and optimization for each platform.
07 — Can I send my own content?
Absolutely. If you want to capture content yourself and have us handle strategy, editing, and posting, we can work with that too. We're flexible depending on what makes sense for your situation.
08 — Do you manage DMs?
Yes, DM and community engagement is included in our full-service management plans. We respond to comments, DMs, and keep your audience engaged on your behalf — all in your brand's voice.
09 — How long until I see results?
Most clients see noticeable increases in engagement and profile activity within 60–90 days of consistent posting. Social media growth is a long game, but with the right strategy and content, momentum builds quickly. We track performance monthly and adjust accordingly.
